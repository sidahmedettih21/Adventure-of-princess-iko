Our Universe

My Ikram, my heart, my princess,

I am profoundly sorry. These past days, I have been consumed by stress and worry, and instead of protecting you from that darkness, I let it spill onto you.

I texted too much, I was not present, and I failed you. I failed to be the safe harbor you deserve. I can only imagine how that must have felt – to have the person who should be your calm become another source of noise and pressure.

You deserve peace, not my chaos. This game is not just a game. It is every apology I cannot fit into words, every moment I wish I could take back, every reminder of how beautiful you are and how lost I am without you by my side. I built this world for you, because my real world only makes sense when you are in it.

I need you to know: I am not okay these days. But I am fighting to be better – for you, for us. My stress is not your burden to carry, and I am learning to handle it so I can be the man you believe I am.

Please forgive me, my love. Not today, not tomorrow – but when your heart is ready. I will wait. I will listen. I will do whatever it takes. I love you more than any game could ever express.

You are my light. You are my spark in the darkness.

Forever yours,
Sidahmed 💜
